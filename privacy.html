Privacy Policy

At RoadMap, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data and what measures we take to keep it secure.

1. Data Collection

We do not sell any of your personal data when you use the RoadMap app. The information we collect is solely for the purpose of providing you with the best user experience and is handled securely. We respect your privacy and will never ask for personal information such as passwords or credit card details.

2. Data Encryption

All user data is encrypted during transmission, ensuring that your information is kept safe from unauthorized access. Even our developers cannot access your data without proper encryption keys, ensuring that your privacy is maintained at all times.

3. No Access to Personal Information

We do not store your email address, location, or any personal information in a way that makes it easily accessible. Our development team does not have direct access to your personal data, and we will not share it with third parties without your consent.

4. Newsletter

RoadMap may send you a newsletter with information about the RoadMap service once you signed up for it (currently the 'join waitlist' button). RoadMap will store your e-mail address on its server to accumulate the mailing list used for the newsletter. You may opt out of receiving the newsletter at any time by reaching out to our email detailed at the end of this policy

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request that we delete your data at any time. If you wish to opt-out or delete your information, please contact us via the contact form on our website.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and you will be notified of significant changes. Please check this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out to us at roadmapbeta@gmail.com.
